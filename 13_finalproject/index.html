Would use a window fan to create the wind, or blowdrier. ### RF detection of drones A mesh network of sensors that can triangulate the location of a drone based on its RF emissions. Would use software defined radios (SDRs) to pick up the signals. Combined with acoustic sensors, maybe electromagnetic in closed spaces ### Electromagnetic based drone navigation in closed spaces. But I would still like to make something that helps me sail better in some way. One thing that always happens to me is that I forget to do something. I once forgot to drop the centerboard, but usually it happens that a rope gets stuck around the corner of my boat and almost flips me. The thing is that I need to look back a lot for this not to happen, and I am usually yapping to friends sitting in the front. The idea is this device I could put on top of my helmet which records in all directions and it knows what to watch out for - basically it is a copilot of mine. I could use it as well to change the music with some voice commands, and it could give me feedback on how good my technique is. I think it would be fun to have a "sticky" version of a camera or some sensor array I could put on the boat - like an accelerometer to tell me what my steepest angle was and if it is optimal. <img src="render.png" class="img-fluid" /> <img src="uglyrender.png" class="img-fluid" /> I tried to get a nicer render with gemini - I think ultimately I want to make it look sleek, and I think I can model it around one of the MIT helmets.
